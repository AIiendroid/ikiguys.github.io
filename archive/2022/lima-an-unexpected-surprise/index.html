Lima ‐ An unexpected surprise

For us, Lima was a dusty horizon, described mostly by outdated guides from 2017. They all, like many of our friends who are hardened mountain tourist now, advised us to avoid Lima. However, some sincerely admired the huge city and told us where to stay and what to see. Now, after spending 3 unforgettable days in Lima, we tell you what our eyes saw, ears heard and why smiles bloomed on our faces. Let's go!
